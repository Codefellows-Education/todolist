How to get started

  • Start by naming your character. Pick an awesome name.
  • Go ahead and add your daily taks. These are tasks that need to be done everyday. i.e. drink coffee.
  • Now you want to add your long-term goals. These are not daily but can be due soon. ie go grocery shopping saturday.
  • For each long-term task, assign a diffuclty level. ie shopping = easy. date-night = hard
  • click the checkbox for each task you completed. The task will move into a completed section.
  • You have the option of permantly deleting the task or leaving it crossed off to see your accomplishements.
  • The more tasks you do, the higher you level up.
  • As you level up, your avatar will change. Everyone starts out as a task peasant.
  • Have Fun as you complete life's tasks.
